About us

   
     Aroma Cafe

   
    This is a start-up company by young aspiring food engineers
    who are specialised in making various kinds of aromatic and 
    energetic coffees. We serve a range of coffees from the finest
    coffee beans, roasted here in our cafe. Our cafe is a perfect 
    "hangout" spot where you can meet new people and make 
    conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups
    of coffee. Our mission is to ensure that every guest experiences
    genuine and sincere customer service that warms their heart 
    and make them feel better. Most importantly "We serve with 
    love and passion".
    Let us flutter your heart.
